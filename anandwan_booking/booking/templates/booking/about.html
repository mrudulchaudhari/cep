About The Project

The Anandwan Visitor Room Booking System was developed to streamline the process of room reservations at Anandwan - "Forest of Joy" - a community rehabilitation center founded by social activist Baba Amte near Warora in Maharashtra, India.

Our system enables visitors to check room availability, make bookings online, and manage their reservations efficiently. This helps Anandwan staff better manage their accommodation resources and provide improved service to visitors including students, researchers, volunteers, and tourists.

This project was developed as part of our college project work with the goal of applying our technical skills to create meaningful solutions for social organizations.
